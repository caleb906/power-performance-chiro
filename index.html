Good Questions

Before you book.

The stuff people usually want to know. If your question isn't here, call the office.

Is this a crack-you-and-out-the-door place? +
No. Every new patient gets a full-body movement evaluation first. We figure out what's actually driving the issue, then build a plan you can stick to. Adjustments are part of it, not the whole thing.
Do you take insurance? +
We're out of network. We provide a superbill you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. Most patients find the trade-off worth it for the time and the results.
How long until I feel better? +
Most patients feel real change in the first one to three visits. Lasting results depend on the issue and how well you hold up your end of the rehab. We'll be honest about timelines after the eval.
I'm not really an athlete. Is this still for me? +
Yes. We treat framers, nurses, parents, anyone who works hard and wants to move better. "Performance" means whatever performance means for you.
