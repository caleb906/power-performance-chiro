FAQ

Common Questions

The stuff people ask before they book.

Is this a "crack-you-and-out-the-door" kind of place?

No. Every new patient gets a full-body evaluation first. We figure out what's actually driving the issue, then build a plan you can stick to. Adjustments are part of it, not the whole thing.

Do you take insurance?

We're out-of-network. We provide a superbill you can submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. Most patients find the trade-off worth it for the time and the results.

How long until I feel better?

Most patients feel real change in the first 1–3 visits. Lasting results depend on the issue and how well you hold up your end of the rehab. We'll be honest about timelines after the eval.

I'm not really an athlete — is this still for me?

Yes. We treat framers, nurses, parents, anyone who works hard and wants to move better. "Performance" means whatever performance means for you.
